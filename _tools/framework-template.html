W3C Impact Framework (draft)

This document describes the current draft of W3C Impact Framework based on approach anchored in the Theory of Change, applied to vision of the Web derived from the W3C Vision and the Ethical Web Principles.

Outcomes are the pre-requisite needed to make the high-level impact statement true; outputs are the specific deliverable and changes W3C is well-positioned to carry forward; activities are the ways these outputs get produced within W3C, based on inputs W3C needs to ensure are and remain available.

Any of those can be associated with indicators that help detect progress or regressions, and where additional focus and resources might be needed.
