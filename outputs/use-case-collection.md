While some groups take more or less formal and systematic approaches to develop use cases, and while explainers (which the W3C TAG strongly encourages as part of developing technologies) are expected to be [anchored in end-user's need](https://w3ctag.github.io/explainer-explainer/#end-user-need), the work on use cases remains ad-hoc and uncoordinated across groups.
